Every app you add is a new risk. One update can silently break another, and without a developer, you're left debugging a broken store during your busiest season.

Every feature you need costs another $30 to $150/month on top of your plan. Most business owners don't realize how quickly that adds up until they're locked in.

Shopify themes are used by tens of thousands of businesses. There is no template that makes your brand look unique, because it was designed for everyone.

App updates, SSL renewals, vulnerability patches, all your responsibility. Skip one and you're either exposed to attacks or paying a developer $150/hour to fix it.

Shopify support handles their platform, not your custom setup. When something breaks during a launch or a sale, you are completely on your own.

As your email list, traffic, and order volume increases, so do your Shopify plan tier, transaction fees, and app costs. Growth should reduce your overhead, not increase it.
